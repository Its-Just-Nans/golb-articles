# Latex

## Good to know

- Font sizing

```tex
\tiny
\scriptsize
\footnotesize
\small
\normalsize
\large
\Large
\LARGE
\huge
\Huge
```

- Line spacing

```tex
\usepackage{setspace}
\begin{spacing}{1.25}
TEXT
\end{spacing}
```

- color

```tex
\usepackage{xcolor}
\textcolor{blue}{example in blue}
```

- center

```tex
\begin{center}
centered
\end{center}
```
